Weekly Training & Flexibility Schedule

Day 1: Upper Body Strength & Hypertrophy + Flexibility

Strength Training

  • Dumbbell Bench Press (SmartBells): 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • One-Arm Dumbbell Rows (SmartBells): 4 sets of 8-10 reps per arm
  • Push-Ups on Dip Bars: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Overhead Shoulder Press (SmartBells): 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Resistance Band Face Pulls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Core: Plank with Shoulder Taps (3 sets of 20 taps)

Flexibility (Post-Workout)

  • Forward Fold: 2 sets of 30 seconds
  • Shoulder Stretch (Puppy Pose): 2 sets of 30 seconds
  • Bridge Pose: 2 sets of 10-15 seconds

Day 2: Lower Body Strength & Power + Mobility

Strength Training

  • Goblet Squats (Kettlebell): 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts (SmartBells): 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats (SmartBells): 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
  • Kettlebell Swings: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Resistance Band Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Flexibility (Post-Workout)

  • Hamstring Stretch: 2 sets of 30 seconds per leg
  • Pigeon Pose: 2 sets of 30 seconds per side
  • Deep Squat Hold: 2 sets of 30 seconds

Day 3: Conditioning & Agility + Flexibility

Conditioning

  • High Knees with Resistance Band: 3 sets of 20-30 seconds
  • Step-Ups (use a sturdy surface with Kettlebell): 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
  • Sprint Intervals: 6-8 x 40 meters (rest 90 seconds between sprints)
  • Kettlebell Deadlift to Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Flexibility (Post-Workout)

  • Spiderman Stretch with Rotation: 2 sets of 5 rotations per side
  • Straddle Stretch: 2 sets of 30 seconds per side
  • Wrist Mobility Drills: 5 minutes

Day 4: Active Recovery + Flexibility Session

Active Recovery

  • Light yoga or walking (20-30 minutes)

Flexibility Session

  • Pike Stretch: 2 sets of 30 seconds
  • Shoulder Dislocates with Resistance Band: 2 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Cat-Cow Stretch: 1-2 minutes
  • Wall Splits: 2 sets of 20 seconds per side

Day 5: Upper Body Hypertrophy + Flexibility

Strength Training

  • Incline Dumbbell Press (SmartBells, elevated surface): 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Weighted Dips (using SmartBells or resistance bands): 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises (SmartBells): 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Resistance Band Rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Core: Ab Wheel Rollouts (or substitute plank variations)

Flexibility (Post-Workout)

  • Shoulder Stretch (Eagle Arms or Puppy Pose): 2 sets of 30 seconds
  • Bridge Pose Progressions: 2 sets of 10-15 seconds
  • Forward Fold: 2 sets of 30 seconds

Day 6: Lower Body Power & Hypertrophy + Mobility

Strength Training

  • Kettlebell Goblet Squats: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Step-Ups with Dumbbells: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
  • Resistance Band Glute Kickbacks: 3 sets of 12-15 reps per leg
  • Sprint Starts: 6-8 x 20 meters

Flexibility (Post-Workout)

  • Hamstring Stretch: 2 sets of 30 seconds per leg
  • Deep Squat Hold: 2 sets of 30 seconds
  • Pigeon Pose: 2 sets of 30 seconds per side

Day 7: Rest or Active Recovery + Flexibility Session

Active Recovery

  • Foam rolling or light cycling (20-30 minutes)

Flexibility Session

  • Straddle Stretch: 2 sets of 30 seconds per side
  • Bridge Pose or Backbend Progressions: 2-3 sets of 10-15 seconds
  • Spiderman Stretch with Rotation: 2 sets of 5 rotations per side
  • Wrist Mobility Drills: 5 minutes
